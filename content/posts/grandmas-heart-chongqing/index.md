Rating: 2

Apparently this is 4 servers.
Each smaller pack is a different combination of ingredients, seemingly ascending/descending as one has just 2 packets.
I'm not sure if it's supposed to be one progressive meal or what.

Pickled mustard, chickpeas, bone soup base, fake meat oil sauce, and spicy sauce pack which is kindof hot oil.
There's a couple of different varieties of meat sauce I think, but only one chickpea and two pickled mustard.
One of the hot oil packets was a different color but I didn't notice anything varied.

The chili is aromatic, and the oils emulsified well.
The taste is okay, not frontal spicy but warming.
Chickpeas and meat are good.
Salt and flavor levels support preparing it dry and adding vegetables.

I'm just not sure what it's supposed to _be_.
Following the packets would give a very wonky experience and there's just not enough of the vegetables.
