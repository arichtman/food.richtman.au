Rating: 2

Veg flake - spring onion and...carrot?
2 x liquid sauce sachets - one for extra heat.
Base chilli sauce very runny and vinegary.
I think it's just the hot ones sauce kinda reformulated and repackaged.
Noodles are bog standard thin wheat.

I had cup version but stove cooked.
Decent heat up front.
Got that chipotle smoke.
Not awful for bargain bin.
Booster heat sauce pack looks orange like habanero.
Boosts the heat, vinegar, and fruitiness.

I enjoyed it but it's cheap noodles with hot sauce, coasting on brand recognition.
