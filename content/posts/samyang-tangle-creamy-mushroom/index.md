Rating: 3

Packets - powder, sauce, and flake.
The noodles are special, wide like fettuccine, though they feel extra stiff?
The flake is mostly dried mushroom, with a little vegetables.
The sauce is glossy and thick but not overly mushroomy.

There's not really any free oil so no emulsion to be had.
The noodles are about what fettuccine would be.
Soup is thin, and tastes like cream of mushroom soup.
It's nice.

Guess you always want what you can't have.
There's probably equivalent or better in Western products but if you're looking for Asian cup-o-pasta/noodles, this is it.
